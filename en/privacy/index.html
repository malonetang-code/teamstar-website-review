PRIVACY

Privacy

This notice applies to the Teamstar Manufacturing / Qunxin Industrial website. We collect only the information needed to review technical RFQs and respond to business enquiries.

Information we collect

When you submit an RFQ or contact us by email, you may provide your name, company, email address, telephone number, product or machine information, technical requirements, drawings and photographs.

How information is used

We use this information to clarify requirements, assess manufacturing feasibility, prepare quotations, respond to enquiries and retain necessary business correspondence. RFQ information is not sold for unrelated advertising.

Forms and technical services

The website RFQ form is transmitted through a third-party form service. The server may retain basic access logs required for security and fault diagnosis. The current website does not deploy advertising or analytics cookies.

Retention and disclosure

Information is retained for the period required for business review and legal obligations. It is not disclosed to unrelated third parties except where needed for RFQ review, website operation or legal compliance.

Contact

To request access, correction or deletion of submitted information, email info@teamstarmfg.com.
