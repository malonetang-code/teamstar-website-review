QUALITY SYSTEM / ISO 9001:2015

Quality control throughout manufacturing

Material verification, in-process inspection, dimensional inspection and final inspection are performed against approved drawings and applicable product requirements.

PROCESS CONTROL

Manufacturing quality control process

Inspection plans are defined according to the product, drawing and operating requirements and cover applicable material, dimensional and edge criteria.

01 / REVIEW

Technical and material review

Confirm the drawing revision, material route and applicable inspection requirements.

02 / IN-PROCESS

In-process inspection

Apply controls during blade shaping, heat treatment, machining and grinding.

03 / FINAL

Final inspection

Verify the agreed material, dimensional and edge requirements.

04 / RELEASE

Batch release

Release products against approved criteria and retain applicable records.

CONTROLLED CHARACTERISTICS

Inspection follows the drawing and process route

Inspection methods are selected for the characteristics that control material identity, heat-treatment condition, mounting fit, blade geometry and applicable surface requirements.

01 / MATERIAL

Material composition verification

Spectrometry or handheld X-ray fluorescence analysis is selected where applicable to the material route and project requirements.

SPECTROLAB spectrometer and VCA handheld XRF spectrometer
02 / HARDNESS

Hardness and metallographic inspection

Vickers hardness, Rockwell hardness or metallographic inspection is applied according to the material, heat-treatment route and controlled requirements.

Mitutoyo MVK-H100, ZHR-8150LK and NIKON metallographic microscope
03 / DIMENSION

Dimensional and geometric inspection

Coordinate, image or profile-projection measurement is selected for mounting interfaces, hole patterns, profiles and other controlled dimensions.

Mitutoyo BEYOND APEX 707, KEYENCE IM-7000 and JT-300
04 / SURFACE

Surface and coating inspection

Surface roughness or coating thickness is checked where required by the drawing, process route or acceptance criteria.

Mitutoyo SJ-310 surface roughness tester and EX-3000 XRF coating thickness gauge
